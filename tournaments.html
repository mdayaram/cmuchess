Tournaments

• UC Fridays February 16th Tournament:

The CMU Chess Club will be holding its first university-wide tournament (20 minutes per person per game). The tournament is free for all participants, and players of all ranks are welcome. There will also be free boards for some non-competitive games. Get in touch with Natalie Komarov (nkomarov@andrew) if you would like to participate, and please specify your approximate level (or USCF rating if you have one) and whether you would like to compete in the tournament or play a game on the side.
